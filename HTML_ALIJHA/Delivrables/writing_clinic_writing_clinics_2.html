Dear colleagues,

This week's patient came in feeling weak, tired, and oddly detached.

Diagnosis: Passive Voice Syndrome.

Compare these two cases:

❌ The report was finalised by the task team.
✅ The task team finalised the report.

Spot the difference?

The first hides the doer; the second puts action front and centre. One drifts, the other drives.

So when you find yourself writing "was completed," "has been undertaken," or "will be implemented," ask: Who's actually doing the work? Then give them the spotlight.
